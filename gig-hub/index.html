The Challenge We Face

Youth Unemployment in the Eastern Cape

Our province has one of the highest youth unemployment rates in South Africa. Many young people have skills but cannot find work opportunities.

Skilled Youth Without Opportunities

Many young people in the Eastern Cape have completed their education or training but cannot find jobs. They have skills in areas like plumbing, tutoring, farming, and deliveries but lack connections to people who need these services.

Work Opportunities Are Hidden

Short-term work opportunities exist in households, farms, schools, and small businesses, but they are mostly shared by word-of-mouth. This means many qualified people never hear about these opportunities.

Digital Divide in Rural Areas

Many people in rural and township communities do not have smartphones or reliable internet access. This digital divide prevents them from using online job platforms that could connect them with work opportunities.

Our Solution

Gig Hub: Connecting People to Local Work

Gig Hub is a simple platform that helps people find short-term work opportunities in their area. We designed it specifically for the Eastern Cape, considering the needs of both educated and less-educated users.

  • Works on any phone - Use our app on smartphones or access through USSD on basic phones
  • Simple to use - No complicated technology, designed for everyone
  • Local focus - Find work opportunities in your own community
  • Safe and trusted - We verify users and include rating systems
  • Multiple languages - Available in English, isiXhosa, and Afrikaans

How It Works

Simple Steps to Find Work or Hire Help

Gig Hub is designed to be easy for everyone, regardless of education or tech experience.

1. Create Your Profile

Tell us about yourself and your skills. You can list what type of work you can do, like plumbing, tutoring, farming, or deliveries.

2. Find or Post Opportunities

Browse available gigs in your area or post a job if you need help with a task. Filter by location, type of work, and date.

3. Connect and Agree

Contact the person directly through the platform. Discuss the job details, timing, and payment before starting.

4. Work and Rate

Complete the job and get paid. Then rate your experience to help build trust in the community.

USSD for Basic Phones

No smartphone? No problem. Dial *134*555# to access Gig Hub on any mobile phone.

Safe and Verified

We verify user identities and include rating systems to ensure safety for everyone using the platform.
